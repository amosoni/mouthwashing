What is Mouthwashing?

Mouthwashing is a narrative-driven first-person psychological horror game set aboard the stranded space freighter Tulpar. Players will follow the crew's eerie and suspenseful journey as their time and resources slowly dwindle.

Captain Curly's failed suicide attempt has doomed everyone to a tragic fate, and now he is disabled and helpless. The crew must decide whether to violate the company's strict ban and open the mysterious cargo hold, which may be their only hope for survival.

In this tense and atmospheric environment, players will face the psychological trauma of isolation and fear of the unknown, with every decision carrying life-or-death consequences.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete the game? ‚ñº

The game takes approximately 3 hours to complete, offering a compact and focused narrative experience. While it's a linear narrative, its surreal visual effects and psychological depth may encourage players to revisit certain scenes.

What is the main gameplay? ‚ñº

Mouthwashing focuses on narrative exploration with minimal combat or survival mechanics, emphasizing storytelling and atmosphere. Main gameplay includes exploring the Tulpar, conversing with crew members, and using items to solve puzzles.

Does the game have multiple endings? ‚ñº

No, Mouthwashing has a single bleak narrative ending focused on the crew's inevitable fate. The game uses non-linear storytelling through disconnected scenes.

What are the system requirements? ‚ñº

Minimum requirements: Windows 7 64-bit, i5-6300HQ processor, 8GB RAM, GTX 560 graphics, DirectX 11, 3GB available storage. Recommended: i5-1135G7 processor, 16GB RAM, GTX 1050 graphics.

Who is this game suitable for? ‚ñº

This game is particularly suitable for players who enjoy psychological horror, sci-fi stories, and deep narratives. The game focuses on atmosphere and psychological horror rather than jump scares, providing unsettling visual effects and thought-provoking storylines.
